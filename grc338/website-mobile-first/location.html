Sharing the Love

Come join us!

Our mission is simple: make people happy. To do this, we actively strive to make the communities around us better, kinder, happier places to be. Whether your school needs a donation for its fundraiser, your team needs a space to celebrate a win, or you need to feed some hungry mouths at your nonprofit’s event—we’ve got your back. While we do not lend our assistance to any specific political or religious groups, we are almost always willing and ready to support local schools and nonprofits with fundraising, special events, and disaster relief initiatives.
